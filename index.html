@geohacker

Sajjad Anwar is a hacktivist and programmer based in Bangalore. He works in the research and design of data infrastructure, analytics and infographics. He hearts maps and often makes one. He is found working with other technologists, social activists and researchers in education, human rights and policy making.

Sajjad is currently helping to build and maintain the data and geographic infrastructure at the Karnataka Learning Partnership, technology partner to the Akshara Foundation. He is also part of the team who is building a collaborative mapping platform for monitoring REDD+ forests and natural resource extraction in Democratic Republic of Congo.

He runs workshops and speaks at conferences ocassionaly.
